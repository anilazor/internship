Creative web design

My web design practice relies on art styles, design theory and years of experience with digital design and art (since 2012) — initially through learning-by-doing photo enhancement and heavy editing, pixel illustrations and vector designs, basic HTML-embedding and then later combined with my studies in frontend design.

I create memorable and intuitive user-centered solutions that are visually appealing while communicating the essence of the topic — always with the intent to convey a strongly recognizable brand identity.

Frontend development

Having chosen to specialize my studies in frontend design, I am able to create highly customized UI/UX solutions.

I enjoy facing the challenges that inevitably comes with coding JavaScript and advanced CSS. Being motivated by an eager to find the best solution, I always seek to expand my knowledge and skillset aligned with the continuous progress of media and technology.

Combining skills

With the privilege of being able to combine my passions for design and coding, I build high quality solutions with no compromises.
That includes meaningful interface designs, clean HTML-semantics, efficient CSS-files and optimized JavaScript.
This way, my solutions deliver high performance both visually and technically.
